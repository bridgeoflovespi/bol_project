Garbage collection activities

6.20.2024

By Vannak

1st Garbage Free Environment Campaign

We have organized a program that guides young volunteers to participate in environmental clean-up under the theme "Environmental Campaign No Garbage" in the 20km clean-up project, starting from the St. Paul Institute dormitory and along National Road 3. Until Tram Kak Market, Takeo Province. This campaign is directly supported by St. Paul Institute, Takeo Provincial Department of Environment, village, commune and district authorities. This clean and garbage-free environment campaign aims to raise awareness among young people on how to manage waste, cultivate courage and solidarity with young people in order to participate in more social development activities. Prosperous and full of human resources, cultivate people of both sexes to love, protect the environment and be a good example for the next generation. We led 44 young volunteers for the first activity (June 3, 2023), 24 for the second time (July 1, 2023), 24 for the third time (June 05, 2023). (August 2023) and 25 for the last time in the first season.
