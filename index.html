Garbage collection activities

Fourth "Litter-Free Environment Campaign" at St. Paul Institute. These are the activities of the fourth "Litter-Free Environment Campaign", the last of the first season of the St. Paul Institute Bridge of Love family. For this fourth campaign, we are cleaning from the St. Paul Institute's dormitory and along the road to the grounds of the St. Paul Institute, which is about 700 meters away.
